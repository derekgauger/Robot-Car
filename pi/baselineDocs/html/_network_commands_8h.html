◆ HORN_PULSE_COMMAND

#define HORN_PULSE_COMMAND   (0x10000000)

This indicates that the command received is a command to pulse the horn. The lowest 12 bits indicate the period for the horn sound in ms. The next 12 bits indicate the time that the horn is to sound in ms. The robot may approximate these periods based upon the periodic task rate selected.

◆ HORN_SOUND_COMMAND

#define HORN_SOUND_COMMAND   (0x20000000)

This command indicates that the horn is to sound continuously.
